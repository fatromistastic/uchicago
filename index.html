Beyond what is visible, is what is (2025)

~4.5 mins — Orchestra

Score (PDF) · Recording

This work explores the interaction between deterministic and stochastic compositional processes, juxtaposing algorithmic structures in OpenMusic with latent-space interpolation via a fine-tuned language model. The quartet’s harmonic architecture is informed by Fourier analyses of harmonic spectra and poetic structures drawn from visionary texts, creating both precision and fragility in sound.

The stone and the dream are made from the same substance (2023)

~7mins — for 2 pianos and 2 percussion

Score (PDF) ·

Listen on SoundCloud

Written for performer Bella Bozic, this piece investigates resonant transformation and the acoustic illusions of bow pressure and harmonic activation. The structure maps Fourier vectors of pitch-class content to physical gesture, allowing timbral states to spiral outward from silence.

Between Visible and Hidden Worlds (2022)

~9′ — Electronics / Fixed Media

This fixed-media composition translates DFT transformations of harmonic spectra into evolving sound fields. Combining generative synthesis, field recording, and algorithmic modulation, it situates sonic material between analytical abstraction and sensory immediacy.
